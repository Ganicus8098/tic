“Global Wellness Day” 2023

Tashkent International Clinic and Spa Global Expert are proud to announce that the 12th Annual Global Wellness Day was held in Uzbekistan.

The Global Wellness Day was founded by Ms. Belgin Aksoy 11 years ago in Turkey. The purpose of the initiative was encourage many countries to support their nations to feel healthy, physically, mentally and emotionally with the slogan – “One day can change your whole life”.

The event was held at Panarams hotel on 10th of June. The Tashkent International Clinic and Spa Global Expert were pleased to welcome Ms. Consuelo Vidal-Bruce – United nations Resident Coordinator to open ceremony as part of UN 17th Sustainable Development Goals – Good Health and Well- Being and wellness expert Mr. Tejedor Cesar, Emeritus Ambassador of Global Wellness Day. The distinguished guests were invited from the embassies, international organizations and national communities to be part of the important event.

We are very happy to inform you that now Uzbekistan is part of the Global Wellness Day map.

“Cleaning Mountains” Campaign

On 29th of May, CSR Committee of AmCham organized the first environmental campaign in 2023 – “Cleaning Mountains Day” in Oqtash. The campaign was dedicated to climate action in commemoration of Earth Day to demonstrate that we all accountable for our current environmental crisis and each of us play a critical role in protecting our planet from pollution.

It was a great initiative of CSR members, Delta Global Solutions and supported by Korzinka, TIC and TIS. The first campaign gathered more than 60 volunteers with children who came to clean the place, to enjoy with landmarks and beautiful waterfalls and to enjoy fighting in competitions with prizes awards.

It was the first but not the last event dedicated to Global Goals project.

CSR Awards Ceremony – 2022!

On October 17, AmCham Uzbekistan held the first CSR Awards Ceremony!

The goal of the award is to raise awareness on CSR projects implemented by our member companies and to highlight the impact they are having on all aspects of society, including economic, social, and environmental.

AmCham Uzbekistan has awarded 15 CSR projects in the environment, women empowerment, care, education, and social innovation categories, among which 5 were implemented as part of the activities of the AmCham CSR Committee. The event officially started with the opening remarks by:

  • Marisa Lago, US Department of Commerce, Under Secretary for International Trade

  • Sodiq Safoyev, First Deputy Chairperson of the Senate

  • Paul Poletes, Chargé d'Affaires, US Embassy Tashkent

  • Oybek Khalilov, AmCham Uzbekistan President

During the opening ceremony, the importance of bilateral relations and investment opportunities between the US and Uzbekistan was emphasized.

The First CSR Award Ball was attended by 250 participants including US delegation, government officials, AmCham members and IFIs representatives. Tashkent International Clinic had an honor to be a sponsor of the ceremony and was awarded in nomination of Environmental initiatives. TIC team and Board support CSR initiatives already 20 years from the establishing of TIHES.

CSR initiative – Green Tashkent

Tashkent International Clinic (TIC) has been actively involved in CSR initiatives since its inception in 1997. Today, Tashkent International Clinic focuses on three general sustainable developments: social, educational and environmental protection activities.

This year, Tashkent International Clinic started with sponsoring the environmental protection project “Green Tashkent” that was initiated by Makhsustrans (the leading waste management company in Uzbekistan) with the purpose to improve the ecosystem of the city.

In April 17th 2021, in cooperation with the AmCham CSR group, TIC participated in an open-door activity, “Clean-up Bolajon Park”, in the center of the city. This event was organized by Makhsustrans and AmCham Uzbekistan and supported by a number of international organizations.

Charity Football Game

On June 15, 2021, agrocluster Silverleafe initiated together with the Professional Women's Football Club “Sogdiana” the “Charity Football Game”. The main purpose if that game was to raise funds for the Charity organization in Uzbekistan “Ezgu Amal” NGO whose mission is to help children with cancer. The Charity Game designed to support the goal of all organizations that seek to support women's empowerment, as well as charitable initiatives. Tashkent International Clinic was the sponsor of that event and transferred 1500$ to the fund of “Ezgu Amal” and therefore was awarded with the certificate of appreciation.
