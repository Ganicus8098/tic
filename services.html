Family Medicine

FAMILY MEDICINE

The Tashkent International Clinic (TIC) is focused on providing outstanding health services for people of all ages. We have a comprehensive medical team with extensive primary care services, addressing all the health needs of expat families.

TIC takes a team approach to caring for you and your family and helps manage your health so you can be at your best. If you have asthma, diabetes, heart disease, or other chronic conditions, we'll help you get ahead of your symptoms so you can live a better life with less worry about what might hold you back.

Languages: All our staff are fluent in English, but we also attend to our patients using Russian, Uzbek, German, Korean, Spanish and French when needed.

Physical Therapy

PHYSICAL THERAPY

Physical Therapy is the branch of the Health Sciences which help patients regain or improve their physical abilities, by utilizing manual therapy techniques and therapeutic exercises. Every Physical Therapy treatment will:

  • Provide an evaluation and treatment of human movement dysfunctions, including muscle, joint and neurological conditions

  • Promote healthy lifestyle

  • Treat the underlying cause of a problem, not just its symptoms

Available Services

  • Back Care: Spinal, back and cervical injuries

  • Sciatic Nerve Treatment: Lower back, hip and leg pain

  • Sports Rehabilitation and Injury Prevention for all playing levels, including the application of functional bandages

  • Joint Conditions: Frozen shoulder, knee injuries, and other associated conditions

  • Pain Management: Acute and chronic pain treatment

Global Posture Rehabilitation: The Mézières Method

Most of the musculoskeletal problems of the human body are caused by daily activities that produce an imbalance in the whole muscular system. Thanks to the Mézières Method, the contracted or shrunken muscles that cause pain and movement dysfunctions can be located and their initial lengths can be restored. This allows the body to regain its harmonious form.

Acupuncture

ACUPUNCTURE AND TRADITIONAL MEDICINE

Acupuncture can assist persons with chronic pain and suffering, lessen their reliance on medicine, and enhance their quality of life. Acupuncture, a kind of traditional Asian medicine, may be helpful for those who have chronic pain and discomfort.

Acupuncture is generally used to alleviate pain caused by a range of illnesses and disorders, including:

  • Allergies

  • Osteoarthritis and arthritis

  • Backache

  • Chemotherapy-induced nausea and vomiting, as well as postoperative nausea and vomiting

  • Dental discomfort after a tooth extraction or dental surgery

  • Sinus, tension, and migraine headaches are all examples of headaches

  • Cramps during menstruation

  • Neck ache

Acupuncture is also used to treat and manage the following conditions:

  • Stress and depression management

  • Acupuncture may aid with reproductive concerns by increasing blood flow, balancing hormones, and relieving stress when used in combination with standard fertility therapies

  • Insomnia

  • Weight loss in addition to a current weight loss program

Nutrition Care

DIET AND CLINICAL NUTRITION

The key to a healthy diet is to eat the right amount of calories for how active you are so you balance the energy you consume with the energy you use. If you eat or drink more than your body needs, you'll put on weight because the energy you do not use is stored as fat. If you eat and drink too little, you'll lose weight.

Nutrition and diet are important aspects of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Nutrition refers to the study of nutrients in food, how the body uses them, and how they affect health.

A healthy diet is one that provides the necessary nutrients to support growth, development, and overall health. It's important to consume a variety of foods from all food groups, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

A balanced diet also involves proper portion control and moderation of foods high in sugar, salt, and unhealthy fats. Nutrition and diet play a crucial role in the prevention and management of chronic diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and obesity. By understanding the principles of nutrition and following a healthy diet, individuals can improve their overall health and well-being.

Gynecology

OBSTETRICS AND GYNECOLOGY

An Obstetrician Gynecologist, commonly known as an OB/GYN, is a medical specialist who focuses on women's reproductive health. They are trained professionals who provide comprehensive care to women during pregnancy, childbirth, and beyond. Additionally, they address various gynecological concerns such as menstrual irregularities, reproductive system disorders, and fertility issues.

  • Obstetrics - pregnancy management: assessment of anamnesis, risks, complications of pregnancy

  • Gynecology - diagnostics, treatment of gynecological diseases, colposcopy

  • Gynecological endocrinology -treatment of infertility, miscarriage, hormonal disorders in different age periods

  • Planning for pregnancy, contraceptive issues

  • Ultrasound examination of the pelvic organs, thyroid gland, mammary glands, small terms of pregnancy
